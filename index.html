"Director's message"

Since the first moment the building blocks were laid for the establishment of Al-Furaih Hospital more than 25 years ago by its founder, the hospital has sought to provide care and privacy for the mother and child. Al-Furaih is considered one of the first private hospitals in the region with a distinguished elite of specialized and experienced doctors. We assure you that we are committed to providing specialized, high-quality medical care that has been prepared to enhance the level of maternal and child care services through optimal exploitation of our available resources.
